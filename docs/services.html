Services

Focused support for organizations that need practical help, not complexity

Services are designed for churches, nonprofits, community groups, and selected small local businesses that need clearer systems, more consistent updates, and support that is useful in day-to-day work.

Service options

Four practical ways I can help

The strongest first engagement is usually the one that solves a real bottleneck quickly: outdated website content, scattered communication, or recurring work without a clear system behind it.

For churches and faith communities

Website Updates & Announcement Support

One-time or recurring support to keep website content, event details, service information, and public-facing updates clear and current.

  • Homepage and key page updates
  • Service time and event information changes
  • Announcements and bulletin uploads
  • Content cleanup and consistency improvements
  • Recurring updates if needed
Best fit when important public-facing information is often outdated or updated too late.
For churches and community groups

Social Media & Public Updates Support

Practical help for organizations that want more consistency in event reminders, public updates, ministry or program communication, and regular posting rhythm.

  • Announcement posts
  • Event reminder posts
  • Special event promotion support
  • Basic content planning
  • Caption writing and posting support
Best fit when communication is happening, but not consistently enough for people to follow.
For nonprofits and organizations

Operations Setup & Workflow Improvement

A practical support engagement to reduce scattered work, improve follow-through, and build simple systems that real teams can maintain.

  • Recurring admin checklists
  • Action trackers and decision logs
  • Folder and file organization improvements
  • Workflow review and practical improvement suggestions
  • Light AI-enabled workflow guidance where appropriate
Best fit when recurring work depends too much on memory instead of simple systems.
For selected small local businesses

Simple Website & Digital Upkeep Support

A practical option for businesses that need a simple online presence, cleaner website updates, or lighter ongoing digital support.

  • Simple website setup or refresh support
  • Page content updates
  • Service and contact information cleanup
  • Basic update support after launch
  • Help keeping the website current
Best fit when the work needed is practical, scoped, and does not require a full agency engagement.

Good first projects

Typical ways support often starts

Most engagements begin with one clear problem instead of trying to fix everything at once.

Website cleanup

Outdated information, missing updates, or inconsistent page content that needs attention.

Announcement consistency

Communication that is happening irregularly or is too hard for people to follow.

Recurring admin structure

Important work that needs simple checklists, trackers, and clearer handoffs.

Best fit when

You need practical, scoped support

  • Your team is active but stretched
  • Updates matter, but consistency is slipping
  • You want help that is clear and manageable
  • You want improvement without unnecessary complexity

Less likely to be a fit

Not a full agency model

  • Large multi-channel marketing campaigns
  • Heavy custom development projects
  • Complex branding programs
  • Big-team agency-style retainers

Next step

Start with a short conversation about what support would help most

A simple email is enough to describe your organization, what feels difficult right now, and whether you need website, communications, or operations support.
