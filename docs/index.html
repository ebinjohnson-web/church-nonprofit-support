About

A practical business built to support churches and nonprofits

Ebin Geo Johnson provides practical operations and digital support for churches and nonprofits, with a focus on workflow improvement, website updates, and communications support.

Ebin is a full-time Project Manager and a Project Management instructor at Lambton College. This work builds on that professional background by bringing practical systems, structure, and follow-through into church and nonprofit settings.

The approach is intentionally lightweight and practical, especially for volunteer-led teams, smaller organizations, and leaders who need dependable support without a large-agency model.

Services

How support is provided

Operations Setup & AI Workflow Improvement

Support for churches and nonprofits that want better organization, clearer follow-through, and smarter use of existing digital tools.

  • Folder and document organization
  • Action trackers and decision logs
  • Recurring admin checklists
  • Annual calendars and key deadlines
  • Workflow improvement recommendations
  • Guidance on Microsoft 365 / Teams or Google Workspace / Meet

Church Website & Communications Updates

Support for churches that need a dependable way to keep website content current, clear, and useful.

  • Website text and content updates
  • Event page updates
  • Bulletin or newsletter uploads
  • Service time and contact information updates
  • Homepage refreshes for announcements
  • Basic content cleanup and organization

Church Social Media Support

Support for churches that want more consistent and organized public communication online.

  • Announcement posts
  • Event reminders
  • Special service promotion
  • Content planning and scheduling
  • Caption writing
  • Simple visual coordination using client-provided materials
