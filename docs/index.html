About

Practical support that helps work move forward

I’m Ebin Geo Johnson, a full-time Project Manager and a Project Management instructor at Lambton College.

I support churches and nonprofits by helping them put simple systems in place so important work does not get lost in the busyness of everyday ministry or operations. My approach is practical and easy to maintain, especially for volunteer-led or small teams.

I work with organizations that want help with improving internal organization, simplifying recurring administrative work, making better use of tools like Microsoft 365 or Google Workspace, keeping websites and communications up to date, and creating more consistency in social media and public communication.

Services

How I can help

Operations Setup & AI Workflow Improvement

I help churches and nonprofits improve organization and follow-through by setting up simple systems, templates, and workflows that are easy to maintain.

  • Folder and document organization
  • Action trackers and decision logs
  • Recurring admin checklists
  • Annual calendars and key deadlines
  • Workflow improvement recommendations
  • Guidance on Microsoft 365 / Teams or Google Workspace / Meet

Church Website & Communications Updates

I help churches keep their website current, clear, and useful.

  • Website text and content updates

  • Bulletin or newsletter uploads

  • Basic content cleanup and organization

Church Social Media Support

I help churches maintain a more consistent and organized social media presence.

  • Announcement posts
